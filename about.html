About RideNow

Connecting riders with drivers to make transportation more accessible, affordable, and reliable for everyone.

Our Mission

RideNow was founded in 2023 with a simple vision: to revolutionize the way people move around in their cities. We believe transportation should be as reliable as running water, everywhere and for everyone.

Our platform connects riders with drivers at the touch of a button, making cities more accessible and opening up more possibilities for riders and more flexible earning opportunities for drivers.

Why Choose RideNow

Reliable Rides

Request a ride and get picked up within minutes, day or night.

Safety First

Driver background checks, real-time trip tracking, and 24/7 support.

Transparent Pricing

Know the price before you ride with no hidden costs or surge markups.

Available Everywhere

Operating in over 50 cities worldwide and expanding rapidly.
